About Us

Rebounce is much more than your ordinary trampoline park, we offer the best of trampoline park experience with a host of incredible indoor adventure attraction options. It is a place that helps you create fabulous memories.

Suspended in mid-air or in the heat of competition, nothing matters but the moment. Once you get inside, you’ll see what we mean. It takes an adventurous soul to experience all of our attractions in one visit. This is the joy of play.

Come refresh your mind and engage your muscles. You will always leave our park feeling better than when you entered.
